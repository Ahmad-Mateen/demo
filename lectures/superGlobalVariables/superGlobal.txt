    Super Globals Variables
    Superglobals are special types of variables because they can be accessed from any scope.
    The accessibility can be from any file, class, or even function without the implementation of any special code segments.
    Superglobal variables are inbuilt and predefined
     // These are the super Globals variables
     $GLOBALS.
1) $_SERVER.
2) $_REQUEST.
3) $_POST.
4) $_GET.
5) $_FILES.
6) $_ENV.
7) $_COOKIE.
